1. Our Commitment

At Aureva, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase. By using our services, you agree to the practices described below.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better service and a seamless shopping experience. This includes:

  • Personal data: name, email address, shipping/billing address, phone number.
  • Payment information: processed securely by our payment partners; we do not store full payment details.
  • Usage data: pages visited, products viewed, and interactions with our site (via cookies).

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie settings in your browser. Essential cookies are required for site functionality, while optional cookies help us personalize content.

You may opt out of non-essential cookies at any time via our cookie consent tool.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to:

  • Process orders and deliver products.
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates.
  • Improve our website, products, and customer support.
  • Send promotional emails (only with your consent).

5. Sharing Your Information

We never sell or rent your personal data. We may share limited data with trusted third parties solely for the purpose of order fulfillment (e.g., shipping carriers, payment processors). All partners are contractually bound to protect your data.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption, firewalls, and access controls, to protect your data. However, no online transmission is 100% secure; we encourage you to also take precautions when sharing information online.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. You can also withdraw consent for marketing emails. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@aureva.com.

  • Request a copy of your data.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Ask us to delete your account and data.

8. Children’s Privacy

Aureva does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly. Please contact us if you believe a child has provided us with personal data.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers, in compliance with applicable data protection laws.
